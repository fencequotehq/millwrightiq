Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. MillwrightIQ is committed to being transparent about how we collect and use information. This policy explains everything in plain language — no legal jargon.

1. Who We Are

MillwrightIQ is a free online toolkit for industrial millwrights and trade apprentices. We are based in Arlington, Texas, United States. You can contact us at: millwrightiq@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate and improve the site:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, tools used, time spent on site — collected automatically via analytics tools

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data independently.

We do not store personal data on our servers. Any analytics data is retained per Google's standard retention policies.

8. Your Rights
